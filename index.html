About Me

🧍‍♂️

I'm a Software Engineer at Jetson, where we're focused on transforming homes across North America by replacing outdated, fossil-fueled systems with advanced electric heat pumps. Previously, I was a Staff Software Engineer at Google and served as a Tech Lead / Manager (TL/M) at both Google and Cash App, building systems that span the full technology spectrum and leading cross-functional teams to deliver high-impact products.

🎓

My journey started with a BASc from Waterloo and MSECE from Georgia Tech, beginning in power systems and control engineering at GE, then gradually moving up the stack - from hardware to firmware to native Android, web systems, and distributed systems. I have held a P.Eng. license from the PEO since 2017.

🚀

As a technical leader, I've architected and scaled distributed systems, led cross-functional product teams, and mentored engineers across multiple levels. I've driven technical strategy and execution for large-scale platforms, managing both technical complexity and organizational alignment to deliver high-impact results.

💻

My technical philosophy centers on getting things working first, then getting them right - so long as we avoid one-way doors. I believe in fast iteration and focusing on high-impact changes. I enjoy applying foundation models to solve hard problems.
